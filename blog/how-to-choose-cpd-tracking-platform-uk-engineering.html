How to choose a CPD tracking platform for UK engineering teams

Choosing a platform is easier when you evaluate workflow fit, evidence quality controls, and rollout risk rather than feature volume alone.

✨ AI Summary

  • Step 1: define employer-side requirements
  • Step 2: evaluate workflow quality, not just feature lists
  • Step 3: run a pilot with measurable KPIs

Step 1: define employer-side requirements

Clarify CPD logging standards, competency mapping needs, and review ownership model.

Step 2: evaluate workflow quality, not just feature lists

Assess how quickly teams can log evidence, complete reviews, and view readiness status.

Step 3: run a pilot with measurable KPIs

Use one business unit and predefined success metrics before wider rollout decisions.

Step 4: plan scale-up and governance

Document standards for data quality, review cadence, and reporting expectations.
